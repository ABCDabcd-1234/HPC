🌟 Viva Questions
❓ Why scaling important?

Neural networks train faster and better with normalized inputs.

❓ Why linear activation?

Because regression predicts continuous values.

❓ Why use MSE?

It penalizes large errors strongly.

❓ What is MAE?

Average absolute prediction error.

❓ Difference between training and testing?

Training learns model, testing evaluates performance.

❓ What is overfitting?

Model memorizes training data and performs poorly on new data.

❓ Why use Adam optimizer?

Fast and adaptive optimization algorithm.
