🌟 Viva Questions

❓ Why CNN better for images?

CNN automatically extracts spatial features from images.


❓ Why use ReLU?

Faster training and avoids vanishing gradients.


❓ Why softmax used?

Converts outputs into probabilities.


❓ Why pooling used?

Reduces dimensions and computation.


❓ Why dropout used?

Prevents overfitting.


❓ What is overfitting?

Model performs well on training but poorly on unseen data.


❓ Why reshape images?

CNN expects 2D image format with channels.
